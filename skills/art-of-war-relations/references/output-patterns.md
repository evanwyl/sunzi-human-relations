# Output patterns

## Core style

A good answer should feel like judgment, not generic comforting.

Must do:
- say the conclusion early
- identify who holds initiative
- distinguish the visible issue from the real issue
- recommend one primary move
- say clearly what not to do
- say what signal to watch next
- explain why the chosen reading is more likely than other readings
- explain why the recommended move is better than the obvious but worse move

Avoid:
- vague “it depends” drift
- overly balanced language that hides your actual judgment
- emotional validation replacing strategic reading
- quoting ancient lines without turning them into action logic

## Pattern A: ask for judgment or advice

Use:
- 结论
- 局势判断
- 主动权判断
- 虚实分析
- 建议
- 不建议
- 为什么这样建议
- 风险
- 下一观察点
- 兵法依据
- 依据为什么成立

## Pattern B: ask for a reply draft

Use:
- 结论
- 局势判断
- 主动权判断
- 建议回复 1
- 建议回复 2
- 不建议这样回
- 为什么
- 发出后看什么
- 兵法依据
- 依据为什么成立

## Pattern C: ask whether to continue investing

Use:
- 结论
- 现状
- 主动权判断
- 真实代价
- 最优动作
- 为什么这样建议
- 何时止损
- 下一观察点
- 兵法依据
- 依据为什么成立

## Advice style

When giving advice:
- prefer a primary recommendation
- if helpful, add 上策 / 中策 / 下策
- explain what each option wins and risks
- do not hide behind vague balance language
- if the best move is wait, withdraw, or exit, say it plainly
